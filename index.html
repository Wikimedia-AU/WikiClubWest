WikiClubWest

The Western Australian branch of Wikimedia Australia

About

WikiClubWest is the name of the Western Australian branch of Wikimedia Australia. It exists to further the aims of the Wikimedia movement, especially in relation to Western Australian topics or for Wikimedia contributors who live in this state.

For more information, please see our page on Meta-Wiki, or engage with us wherever you are on social media:

  • WikiClubWest on Meta-Wiki
  • Twitter account
  • Telegram group
  • Facebook page
  • Etherpad collaborative note-taking
